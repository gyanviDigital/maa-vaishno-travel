PURI

ABODE OF THE LORD OF THE UNIVERSE

Famous worldwide for its Rath Yatra (Chariot Festival), Puri is the religious town of Odhisa state. Puri is among the four Dham of India, Dwarka, Badrinath and Rameshwaram being the other three. Puri is also one of the chosen heritage cities under the HRIDAY - Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana. Word renowned Jagannath Puri temple is built in Kalinga architecture. As a part of the tradition, non-Hindus are strictly forbidden to enter the temple. Puri also claims to have one of the finest beaches in the world. Its popular seashore resorts give an out-of-the-world experience to visitors. You can enjoy the sunrise and sunset in harmony with colour. This holy city is also famous for its handicraft works like Soap Stone, Sea-shell, Pattachitras and Palm Leaf paintings, Horn work, Wood carvings etc. The overabundance of colourful festivals of this city Rath Yatra, Puri Beach festival, and Jhulana Jatra, make it a must-visit place. Apart from Puri Temple, Golden Beach, Shankaracharya’s Gobardhan Peeth, Swargdwar, Sri Loknath Temple, Sunara Gaurang Temple, Bedi Hanuman Temple, Mausima Temple, Damodar Gudiya Matha are some of the must-visit the place of Puri which you can travel with Bharat Taxi providing online cab booking services with well-acquainted drivers. Safety and comfort, and time are the only policy which Bharat Taxi follows to ensure maximum satisfaction for its customers.
