Bharat Taxi arranges for cheap and accessible local taxi hiring facilities for the travelers who wants to rent car for in city usage. Outstation Travels:

If you’re planning for weekend getaways or for a family leisure trip outside the city limits Bharat Taxi arranges for a safe and timely transfers to any city you want to travel. We take up group packages from a city in India connecting places nearby. We would send cars accommodating the size of the group. Outstation car rental service is further divided into three type’s services like: Roundtrip, One Way Trip and Multicity.

Corporate Travel Package:

Bharat Taxi understands the value of your time. Keeping this in mind, we have a special package for businessmen and corporate sector. If you are travelling on business purpose, we can arrange a premium and luxury car suiting your needs right from your arrival till departure. Long Term Rentals:

This service is best suitable when you need to hire a car for more than one week. We have a suitable package for one week, one month or more than that period. With long term rental plans, you don’t have to crawl over again and again for taxi hiring. You can keep it as long as you need and charges will be billed automatically at the end of each month. With Bharat Taxi you will get 24 X 7 on road assistance along with online assistance.
