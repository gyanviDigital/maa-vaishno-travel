Pune, a vibrant city situated in Maharashtra, pulsates with history, culture, and scenic beauty. Whether you're a history buff, a nature enthusiast, or a foodie on a mission, Pune has something to offer everyone. And to make your exploration attractive and comfortable, Bharat Taxi is your perfect partner on this trip!

Historical Delights:

  • Shaniwar Wada: Dive into Pune's royal past with a visit to the majestic Shaniwar Wada, the grand seat of the Maratha Empire. Take a Bharat Taxi and explore its magnificent architecture, remnants of a glorious era.

  • Aga Khan Palace: Situated amidst lush greenery, the Aga Khan Palace offers a glimpse into Pune's architectural heritage. This majestic palace, now a museum, can be easily reached by Bharat Taxi. Whether riding with your family or friends, it will provide full pleasing moments.

Spiritual Solace:

  • Osho Ashram: Seek inner peace at the Osho Ashram, a renowned centre for meditation and spiritual exploration. A Bharat Taxi ride can take you there conveniently, allowing you to begin your spiritual journey refreshed.

  • Kasba Ganapati Temple: Immerse yourself in the city's spiritual heart at the Kasba Ganapati Temple, one of the oldest and most revered Ganesh temples in Pune. Your Bharat Taxi driver can navigate the bustling streets and ensure a smooth journey to this holy site.

Nature's Escape:

  • Sinhagad Fort: For the adventurous soul, a trek up Sinhagad Fort offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city. After conquering the fort, hop back into your Bharat Taxi and unwind on the way back.

  • Lavasa: Craving a scenic getaway? Lavasa, a hill station near Pune, promises a refreshing escape with its waterfalls, lakes, and serene environment. Let Bharat Taxi handle the twists and turns, allowing you to soak in the natural beauty.

Foodie Paradise:

  • Laxmi Road: Embark on a culinary adventure through Laxmi Road, a haven for street food lovers. Sample the local delicacies from savoury misal pav to melt-in-your-mouth shrikhand while your Bharat Taxi waits nearby.

  • Koregaon Park: For a more upscale dining experience, head to Koregaon Park, dotted with trendy cafes and fine-dining restaurants. Your Bharat Taxi can drop you right at the doorstep of your chosen eatery.

Beyond the List:
