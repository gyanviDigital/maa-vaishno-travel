Places to Explore with Mumbai City Cabs

Mumbai, the city that never sleeps, throws its enthusiastic energy at you from the moment you set foot here. From historical landmarks to architectural marvels and religious sites to busy markets, Mumbai has lots of things for everyone. And what better way to explore this dynamic city than with a comfortable Bharat Taxi by your side?

Here are some of the must-visit places in Mumbai that you can easily explore with a Bharat Taxi:

  • Shree Siddhivinayak Temple: One of the most popular Hindu temples in India, dedicated to Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles. Visitors from all over the country visit here daily in thousands. Be prepared for crowds, but the blessings you receive are said to be worth it!
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CST): Formerly known as Victoria Terminus, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is an architectural marvel, showcasing Victorian Gothic Revival architecture at its finest. It's also one of the busiest railway stations in India, so you can experience the pulse of the city here. While visiting with family or friends, it may be one of the best point specially in the evening.

  • Taj Mahal Palace: This iconic luxury hotel has been a witness to Mumbai's history for over a century. Even if you can't stay here, it's worth seeing this architectural marvel.
  • Gateway of India: The majestic archway, built to commemorate the arrival of King George V and Queen Mary in 1911, is a symbol of Mumbai. Take a ferry to Elephanta Caves from here for a dose of history and culture.
  • Global Vipassana Pagoda: The world's largest dome-shaped stupa, built to promote world peace. It's a serene place to meditate or simply take in the architecture.
  • Bandra-Worli Sea Link: It is the marvel of engineering and a cable bridge that stretches across the Arabian Sea. It offers stunning views of the Mumbai skyline, especially at night.
  • Haji Ali Dargah: A beautiful mosque and tomb dedicated to Saint Haji Ali. Located on an islet off the coast of Worli, it's a serene place offering a mix of religious and cultural significance.

These are just a few of the many places you can explore with Bharat Taxis in Mumbai. With its comfortable cars, experienced drivers, and affordable rates, Bharat Taxi is the perfect way to get around the city and see the sights at your own pace.
