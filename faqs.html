Frequently Asked Questions

Bookings.

How Can I Book / Hire A Car From Bharat Taxi?

Booking a car for hiring is very easy in Bharat Taxi.You can book a car on rent either online by the search box provided at the home page or you can call us at our 24 X 7 customer care number +91-9696000999.

Can I Have The Confirmation Of The Vehicle I Want?

Yes. A mail will be sent to your mail id you have provided containing the details of vehicle including color, vehicle number and category.

Can I Book For Any Period Of Time?

Yes. Bharat Taxi provides our customers a wide range of packages to choose from. From half day booking (limited to 40 kms) to Long term rentals you can book for any period of time.

What If I Need To Modify Or Extend My Reservation?

Please dial +91-9696000999 a 24 X 7 customer care and we will work with you to ensure your schedule is accommodated provided that a vehicle is available at that time. If your reservation has already started, you can use bharattaxi.com to modify the reservation. Simply go to your "Accounts" page and make the change through the normal reservation system.

How Do I Cancel A Reservation?

Reservations can be cancelled from our website or you can call our customer care number +91-9696000999to cancel your reservation. You can manage a reservation on your Accounts page.This is where you have the option to cancel your reservation.

Do I Need To Register On Bharat Taxi Before Booking A Car?

No. You can use our service fully without the need to register. Only you need to provide your details at the time of booking.

Payments.

How Can I Pay For My Rental ?

You can pay for your rental by Cash to The Driver.

Can I Get A Bill / Copy Of My Payment?

Yes. After payment you will get a copy of bill duly signed and stamped from our driver.

What Is Peak Season? Are The Prices Different During Peak Season?

Yes. Hourly rates are different during the peak season. But to avoid problems it is suggested by Bharat Taxi that you should book in advance.

If I Cancel My Reservation, Do I Still Have To Pay?

There are no charges if you cancel more than 24 hours before booking start time. (Transnational charges will be applicable on customer.)

What Modes Of Payments Are Accepted?

Presently Bharat Taxi accepts payment in cash and online with debit card/credit card and wallet which is accepted in our gateway.

What Are Other Exclusive Charges Do I Have To Pay?

For outstation, exclusive charges includes Toll Tax, Parking Charges, Night Allowances (will be applied only after 10 pm).
